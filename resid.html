Excel Version

Here is my top secret model. Blue numbers in the excel spreadsheet indicates an input field.

Instructions (Line By Line):

Replace blue numbers for values below. Output data will appear in the "Valuation Model Outputs" section.

Revenue - Revenue for Current Year.
% Growth - Predict revenue growth percentage for the next 10 years.
% Gross Profit Margin - Predict gross margin percentage for the next 10 years.
Operating Expenses - Predict dollar amount of operating expenses for the next 10 years.
Interest Expense/Other Expenses - Predict interest/other expenses to arrive at net income for the next 10 years.
Net Income - Net income for Current Year. Net incomes for Year 1 through Year 10 are calculated.
Book Value - (Assets - Liabilities) at the end of Current Year.
Number of Share Outstanding - Ending shares outstanding for Current Year.
% Change - Predict stock buybacks or dilution for the next 10 years. Optional as it does not affect the predicted stock price.
Current Stock Price - Company's current stock price. Optional as it does not affect the predicted stock price.
Competitor PE Ratio - A competitor's PE ratio. Optional as it does not affect the predicted stock price.
Effective Tax Rate - Company's effective tax rate. 19% is the default value. Estimated by "eyeballing" 2023 earnings. Effective tax rate can vary widely from company to company.
Equity Capital Opportunity Cost - Desired rate of return on equity. 10% is the default value. Estimated by looking at 30 year S&P 500 market returns. CAPM can also be used.
Terminal Growth Rate - Terminal growth rate of business at Year 10. 5% is the default value. Estimated by looking at Nominal GDP growth rates in 2023.
Annual Augmented Payout Ratio - Percentage of annual net income (if positive) that goes to buybacks or dividends. 0% is the default value. It is lower for new/tech companies.
